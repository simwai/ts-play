## 🔬 The Philosophy of Seamless Interaction

We observed that traditional mobile coding environments often felt like trying to perform surgery with oven mitts. The friction between human intent and machine interface was too high! 🌋

So, we evolved. TSPlay now harnesses the power of **Monaco Editor**, re-engineered for a mobile-first world. We've added:

- 📱 **Adaptive Touch Interfaces:** Fluid swipe-to-change tabs that respect your digital flow.
- ⚡ **ATA (Automatic Type Acquisition):** Your imports aren't just strings; they're dynamic links to the vast npm ecosystem. Just import, and watch the types materialize from the ether.
- 🎨 **Aesthetic Harmony:** Choose from a curated selection of themes, from the cozy **Catppuccin** variants to the focused **GitHub** and **Monokai** aesthetics.

## ✨ Features that Spark Joy

- **Integrated WebContainers:** A genuine Node.js runtime. No clouds, no servers, just your local browser performing miracles.
- **Deep Type Insight:** Hover or position your cursor to reveal the hidden structures of your code. Type information is always a thought away.
- **Auto npm Magic:** As you weave your code, TSPlay detects your dependencies, installs them in your local virtual filesystem, and fetches their typings automatically.
- **Persistent Persona:** Your settings, themes, and code snapshots are preserved across the temporal gap of browser refreshes.
- **tsconfig.json Mastery:** Total control over your compilation parameters, with real-time validation and auto-formatting.
- **Share the Vision:** Instantly share your snippets with the world via compressed, decentralization-friendly URLs.

_Note: The development server is pre-tuned with the necessary COOP/COEP headers to stabilize the WebContainer's quantum state._

## ⚙️ Stabilization in Production (Apache / Plesk)

To maintain the integrity of SharedArrayBuffers in the wild, you must ensure your production environment broadcasts the correct isolation signals:

- `Cross-Origin-Embedder-Policy: require-corp`
- `Cross-Origin-Opener-Policy: same-origin`

**Plesk Harmonization:**
Navigate to **Hosting Settings -> Additional headers** and inject these Custom headers manually. It's essential for the WebContainer to reach its full potential.
